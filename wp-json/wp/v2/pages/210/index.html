Education<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Resource Outreach<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

At the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, the MIM developed free virtual lesson plans and other learning resources for educators. These cover a wide variety of topics relating to culture and music and span grades K-12 (depending on the subject matter and content difficulty). The MIM also hosts free professional development sessions for teachers and also offers educators free admission to the museum on these days. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
